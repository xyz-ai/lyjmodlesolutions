Development process

A straightforward path from inquiry to mold delivery.

01

Drawing or sample submission

The project starts with a 2D drawing, 3D file, product sample, or clear reference photos.

02

Quotation and DFM review

The team reviews part structure, project targets, and key production considerations before final quotation.

03

Mold design

After order confirmation, mold structure and development details are arranged for production.

04

Mold manufacturing

Machining, assembly, and internal follow-up move the mold into the trial stage.

05

Trial and adjustment

Trial molding helps confirm part condition, fit, and required adjustments before delivery.

06

Delivery or follow-up production

After approval, the mold can be shipped or used for follow-up injection manufacturing support.

What clients can provide

Helpful inquiry information for faster mold evaluation.

A complete technical package is ideal, but not mandatory. The team can still start from limited information if the product direction is clear enough.

  • 2D drawings or dimension sketches
  • 3D files such as STEP, IGS, X_T, or similar formats
  • Product photos or physical samples
  • Size, weight, material, cavity, and output expectations
  • Packaging, appearance, or assembly requirements when relevant
