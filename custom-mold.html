Development Process

From drawing review to mold delivery.

01

Drawing or sample

Send a 2D drawing, 3D file, sample, or clear reference photos.

02

Quotation and DFM

We review the part structure and discuss practical mold and production points.

03

Mold design

After confirmation, the mold structure and development plan move forward.

04

Manufacturing

Machining, assembly, and internal follow-up bring the mold into the trial stage.

05

Trial

Trial molding confirms the product condition and required adjustments.

06

Delivery

After approval, the mold can be delivered or used for follow-up production.

What Clients Can Provide

Helpful information for faster review.

Complete files are helpful, but not required. We can also start from sample photos or basic size information if the product direction is clear.

  • 2D drawings or clear dimension sketches
  • 3D files such as STEP, IGS, X_T, or similar formats
  • Product photos or physical samples
  • Size, weight, material, cavity, and output expectations
  • Appearance, packaging, or assembly requirements when relevant
